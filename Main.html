Our Objective

We aim to provide organisations all over Africa with the funds needed to continually exercise their services and ultimately stop poaching all together.

We will be successful when the populations of all animals on the endangered species list have been returned to sustainable numbers in the wild. We know thi will take time, and as such, we need as muchsupport as possible from all avenues. Every contribution counts.
